CFP Submissions

The BSidesNYC 0x06 CFP is now closed. Thank you to everyone who submitted!

We accepted submissions for two speaking tracks and one workshop track:
  • Technical - This is our primary programming for the conference with topics ranging from red teaming to privacy policy, DFIR to malware dev.
  • Workshop - Two-hour session where you lead attendees on hands-on exercises.
  • Entrepreneur - Tell the world about what you learned from running/growing a security company.
